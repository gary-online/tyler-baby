Specialized Extension Techniques

Each method is carefully selected to match your hair type and desired results

K-Tip Extensions

Premium keratin bonded extensions that blend seamlessly with your natural hair. Durable, long-lasting, and virtually undetectable.

  • Long-lasting results
  • Natural movement
  • Heat resistant
Most Popular

Beaded Weft

Damage-free method using micro beads to secure wefts. Perfect for adding volume and length without heat or adhesives.

  • No heat or glue
  • Reusable extensions
  • Scalp-friendly

I-Tip Extensions

Individual strand extensions applied with micro beads. Offers maximum flexibility and natural-looking results.

  • Customizable placement
  • No adhesives
  • Easy maintenance

Tape Extensions

Quick application method using medical-grade tape. Thin, lightweight, and comfortable for everyday wear.

  • Fast application
  • Lay flat design
  • Reusable

V-Light Extensions

Advanced technique for ultra-fine hair. Lightweight and virtually invisible, perfect for delicate hair types.

  • Ultra-lightweight
  • Fine hair friendly
  • Invisible bonds
